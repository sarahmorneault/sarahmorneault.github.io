PIANO LESSONS FOR AGES 6+



A Little About Me

I'm a Business Administration student at the University of Maine at Orono. Music has been a part of my life since I was ten years old and I love sharing my musical ability with others! I took piano lessons in 6th grade, but about two years later my teacher moved away and I was left teaching myself new techniques and songs. My teaching styles are age relevant, engaging, and adapted individually for each student. The goal is to give students a basic understanding of how to read music and teach them how to perform piano pieces at their own individual level while making learning fun and entertaining!

~Sarah Morneault
